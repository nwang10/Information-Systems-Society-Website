IS Career Paths

Information Systems opens doors to a wide range of careers

Technology Consulting

Help organizations implement and optimize technology solutions. Work with firms like Deloitte, Accenture, EY, and PwC.

Business / Data Analytics

Turn data into actionable insights. Roles span from business analyst to data scientist across every industry.

IT Project Management

Lead technology projects from planning to delivery. Coordinate teams, manage budgets, and drive outcomes.

Cybersecurity

Protect organizations from cyber threats. Roles include security analyst, risk management, and compliance.

Product Management

Drive product strategy and roadmap at tech companies. Bridge the gap between engineering, design, and business.

Enterprise Systems (ERP)

Implement and manage systems like SAP, Oracle, and Salesforce that power global businesses.
